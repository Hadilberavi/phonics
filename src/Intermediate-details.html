2-5 Age Group

Intermediate Phonics Course

Strengthen your language skills through simple reading texts, common daily expressions, and basic English grammar. This level helps learners develop better comprehension, improve communication, and reinforce their acquired language skills

What Your Child Will Learn

  • Consonant digraphs (ch, sh, th, wh)
  • Vowel combinations and long vowel sounds
  • Reading words with blends and clusters
  • Understanding simple sentence structure
  • Developing reading fluency and speed

What's Included

  • 20 Engaging lessons
  • Interactive word games
  • Beginner readers & stories
  • Personalized learning path
  • Achievement badges & rewards
